// Passenger Log — 003

Hobbies & Interests

✈️

Aviation — A Lifelong Passion

From the moment I saw my first aircraft take off, I was completely hooked. Aviation isn't just a hobby for me — it's a calling. I don't hold a PPL or CPL yet, but that's exactly where I'm headed. Every hour I log in the simulator, every approach I study, every ATC transmission I listen to is a step closer to the cockpit. My dream is to earn my wings and one day fly for real. The sky isn't the limit — it's just the beginning.

🎮

Flight Simulation

Microsoft Flight Simulator and X-Plane are my second homes. I've built a home cockpit with multi-monitor setup, rudder pedals, and HOTAS controls — because why fly one way when you can fly every aircraft ever made?

📡

Plane Spotting & ATC

Armed with a scanner and a flask of coffee at the fence line. I track live ATC feeds on LiveATC.net and can identify most commercial airframes by silhouette alone. There's something meditative about watching metal tubes punch through clouds.

🌍

Travel & Exploration

Every destination is an excuse to experience different airports, different aircraft, and different skies. I travel whenever possible — window seat, always — and photograph approaches and departures.

📚

Aerospace Reading

From Shattered Air to The Right Stuff — aviation literature, accident investigation reports, and aerodynamics textbooks fill my shelves. Understanding failures is how we build safer skies.
